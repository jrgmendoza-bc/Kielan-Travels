Travel Reminders

  • Bring a valid government-issued ID on travel day.
  • Follow call time to avoid route delays for the whole group.
  • Prepare personal medicine and essentials in advance.
  • Pack light and bring weather-appropriate clothing.
  • Check destination weather before your trip.
  • Follow guide and coordinator instructions during tours.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a package?

Send your preferred package, date, and number of travelers through our booking form or Facebook page.

Is there a down payment?

Down payment requirements depend on package type, travel date, and supplier arrangements shared during confirmation.

Can we customize our itinerary?

Yes. We can adjust stopovers and pacing depending on route limits, weather, and schedule availability.

Are transportation and accommodation included?

Package inclusions vary. Each itinerary clearly shows what is included before final confirmation.

What should we bring?

Bring valid ID, personal medicine, weather-ready clothing, and enough cash for optional fees and meals.

Can groups request private tours?

Yes. Private group tours can be arranged based on schedule availability and destination requirements.
